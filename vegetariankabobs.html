  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp dried mustard powder
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard

Cut tofu and all veggies except tomatoes into small bite sized pieces. Mix oil, honey, dry mustard and dijon mustard together. Skewer veggies and tofu.
Brush kabobs with oil mixture. Grill until veggies are soft turning during grilling
